About "Served by pod:"

You might notice a unique message at the top of every page stating "Served by pod: POD_NAME". This showcases the dynamic nature of RHEL OS. Each time you access a page, it might be served by a different instance in the RHEL cluster. These instances represent the reliability and load-balancing capabilities of RHEL, ensuring high availability and seamless performance.
